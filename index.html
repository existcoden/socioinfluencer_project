Process for Influencers

Sign Up
Create an account by completing the sign-up process on the website and register yourself as an influencer with Socio Influencer. Our team will contact you for further procedures after completing the sign-up process
Influencer Agreement
After signing up, the influencer sends over their social media stats and other details, such as the target audience. Once we analyze these stats, an agreement is made between the agency and the influencer.
Assigning the Campaign
According to the respective niches, we send brand campaigns to the selected influencers so they can create content that brands will endorse on social media and reach the target audience.
Delivery
Influencers deliver the content to us, which is then approved by our team of experts and the brand. Once the content is approved, the influencers post it on their respective social media platforms.
